Rooms & Apartments Dragana Nikolić

Private accommodation is located just a few hundred meters from the Geyzir special rehabilitation hospital in Sijarinska Banja.
Dragana Nikolić's private accommodation, rooms and apartments, with its long-standing tradition, has become the main brand for all those who want to feel at home.

Excellent price-quality ratio. Considering that the accommodation is located in the very center of the spa, there are also numerous sights that can be visited on foot. The most significant landmarks are the Church of St. Elijah, mineral springs, swimming pools and the geyser itself.

The ground floor and first floor of the house, as well as the log cabin itself, were completely renovated.

Available on the ground floor: 2 double rooms with shared bathroom, kitchen and terrace, 1 studio apartment with 3 beds with separate bathroom, kitchen and terrace.

On the first floor of the house there are: 2 double rooms and 1 single room with shared bathroom and kitchen, and 1 studio with 3 beds with shared bathroom and kitchen.

There is a log cabin in the yard in front of the house.

There is a huge terrace in front of the house, with a view of the hotel, the church and the park across the street from the house.
